About Us

About The Chronicle

The Chronicle is more than just a news outlet; it's a chronicle of our times. We are dedicated to delivering accurate, unbiased, and in-depth reporting that goes beyond the headlines and delves into the heart of the stories that shape our world.

Founded in 1950, The Chronicle has a rich history of serving our community with integrity and dedication. Our team of experienced journalists is passionate about bringing you the news you need to know, from local happenings to global events.

Mission

  • To chronicle the world around us with accuracy, depth, and integrity, empowering our community with the information they need to be informed citizens and engaged participants in our shared future.

  • To provide a platform for diverse voices and perspectives, fostering understanding and dialogue on the critical issues of our time.

  • To hold power accountable and champion the truth, regardless of personal gain or political pressure.

Vision

  • To be recognized as the leading source of trusted, independent news in Philippines, known for our commitment to journalistic excellence and innovation.

  • To create a vibrant and engaged community where information is accessible, voices are heard, and critical thinking is encouraged.

  • To inspire positive change by empowering individuals with the knowledge and understanding they need to make informed decisions and advocate for a better future.
